## What is Giza Integrations?

Giza Integrations lets partners add DeFi yield optimization to their applications. There are two approaches:

1. **Agentic Integration** -- Giza creates self-custodied smart accounts, handles execution, and runs optimization on your behalf
2. **IaaS (Intelligence as a Service)** -- Use Giza's Optimizer as a stateless service while keeping your own execution infrastructure

Pick the approach that fits your setup. Giza works with Aave, Compound, Morpho, Moonwell, Fluid, and other DeFi lending protocols.

## How It Works

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Agentic Integration">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create Smart Account">
        Giza generates a self-custodied smart account for your user's wallet. This becomes the deposit
        address for agent-managed funds.
      </Step>
      <Step title="User Deposits">
        User deposits USDC or other supported tokens to their smart account address.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Activate Agent">
        Activate the agent with selected protocols and your constraints. Giza's agent starts autonomous optimization.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Automatic Rebalancing">
        The agent continuously monitors APRs and automatically rebalances capital
        across protocols for maximum yield.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Monitor & Withdraw">
        Track performance in real-time. Users can withdraw partially or fully at any
        time.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="IaaS (Intelligence as a Service)">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Call Giza Optimizer">
        Request optimization: send current allocations, capital, and protocols to Giza's Optimizer API.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Receive Intelligence">
        Get optimal allocation, action plan, expected APR improvement, and execution-ready calldata.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Apply Your Logic">
        Decide whether to execute based on YOUR criteria (APR threshold, gas costs, risk rules).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Execute with Your Infrastructure">
        Use the provided calldata to execute rebalancing with your own execution engine.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Use Cases

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Agentic Use Cases">
    ### DeFi Wallets & Exchanges
    
    Add yield generation to user wallets. Agents handle the optimization automatically.
    
    ### Yield Aggregators
    
    Use Giza agents as an automated yield source alongside your existing strategies.
    
    ### Portfolio Apps
    
    Offer users yield optimization without requiring DeFi expertise or manual rebalancing.
    
    ### Neobanks & Fintech
    
    Offer DeFi-powered yield as a savings feature, with no user management required.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="IaaS Use Cases">
    ### Treasury Management
    
    Optimize DAO or protocol treasuries using Giza's intelligence with your custom execution logic and risk controls.
    
    ### Existing DeFi Platforms
    
    Add allocation intelligence to your capital management while keeping execution control.
    
    ### Institutional Asset Managers
    
    Plug optimization intelligence into trading systems with custom risk parameters.
    
    ### Vault Protocols
    
    Use Giza's Optimizer to inform rebalancing decisions in your vault strategies while maintaining your execution infrastructure.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
